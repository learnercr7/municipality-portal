About the Business

The Municipality Service Request Portal is a community-focused website concept designed to support residents when reporting common local infrastructure issues. The portal emphasises clarity, usability, and accessibility so that users can submit well-structured requests without confusion.

Our approach

  • Simple: plain language and clear layout
  • Inclusive: accessibility features such as skip links, ARIA, and strong contrast
  • Efficient: forms with validation and user feedback

Website structure

The website includes pages for business information, services, testimonials, a media gallery, and a contact page featuring an HTML form with validation and feedback.
