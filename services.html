Services and Request Types

Below are examples of common services residents may report through this portal.

Roads

Potholes, damaged road markings, unsafe surfaces, and signage concerns.

Streetlighting

Broken lights, flickering lamps, or areas with insufficient lighting.

Waste

Overflowing bins, missed collections, or illegal dumping reports (non-emergency).

Parks & Public Spaces

Broken equipment, maintenance needs, damaged paths, or safety concerns.

Note: This is a student prototype and does not connect to a council database. Requests sent via the form are for demonstration only.
